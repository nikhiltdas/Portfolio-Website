ABOUT

// bio.txt

I'm a maker, hardware enthusiast, and STEM educator on a mission to make technology tangible for young minds. Currently building innovative programs at Hownwhy Education in Bangalore, I specialize in hands-on, applied learning that bridges the gap between curiosity and competence.

My approach isn't textbook — it's breadboard. I believe children learn best when they're troubleshooting real circuits, iterating on broken prototypes, and discovering that failure is just the first revision. From 3-year-olds exploring basic machines to 2nd graders wiring their first electronics projects, I design experiences that stick.

Previously, I served as Assistant Professor at Mangalam College of Engineering, Ettumanoor, where I taught at the undergraduate level before pivoting fully into early childhood STEM innovation.
